Traffic

By Air:

Huangshan Airport (黄山机场), also known as Tunxi Airport (屯溪机场), is located in the Tunxi suburb of Huangshan City, approximately 7 km from the city center. The airport offers domestic flights to major cities like Beijing, Shanghai, Xi’an, Tianjin, and Guangzhou, as well as international flights to Seoul and Incheon in South Korea. The flight duration is about three hours from Beijing, one hour from Shanghai, two hours from Xi'an, and one and a half hours from Guangzhou. The flight from Hong Kong to Huangshan takes two hours, and from Seoul, around four hours.

Bus:

  1. Walk 200 meters (about 3 minutes) to the Huangshan Airport Terminal bus stop.
  2. Take the 219 bus toward the Train Station Front Square. Operating hours: 07:25-17:55. The fare is 1 CNY.
  3. Get off the bus at the China Life Insurance stop, after passing the Xiangming Hotel North Gate.
  4. Walk 400 meters (about 6 minutes) to Huangshan Mountain Parkview International Hotel.

Taxi:

The taxi service is provided on the first floor of the terminal building in the airport. A ride to the city center costs about 15~20 CNY in 10 minutes.

By Train:

Huangshan City is served by one high-speed railway station, Huangshanbei Railway Station (黄山北火车站). It connects to major cities across China, including Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, and Guangzhou. The journey from Beijing takes about 5.5 hours, while the train from Shanghai takes 2.5 hours, and from Hangzhou, 1.5 hours.

Bus:

  1. Walk 150 meters (about 3 minutes) to the Gaotie BeiZhan bus stop.
  2. Board the Gaotie Express 2 bus heading towards Crown Plaza Hotel. The fare is 5 CNY.
  3. Exit the bus at the Xiangming Hotel stop, after passing Xiushui Haoyuan.
  4. Walk 400 meters (about 6 minutes) to Huangshan Mountain Parkview International Hotel.

Taxi:

The taxi service is also provided, which costs about 45~70 CNY in 25 minutes.

Tips:

  • Google Maps is not available in China, so please use Baidu Map, AMap, or Apple Maps instead.

  • For payments on buses, trains, and taxis, you can use Alipay or WeChat.
