Traffic

By Air:

Huangshan Airport (黄山机场) is also called Tunxi Airport (屯溪机场), located in the Tunxi suburb of Huangshan City, 7km from the city center. The airport has domestic flights from Beijing, Shanghai, Xi’an, Tianjin, and Guangzhou, and international flights from Seoul and Inchon in South Korea. It takes about three hours from Beijing, an hour from Shanghai, two hours from Xi'an and about an hour and a half from Guangzhou. The flight from Hong Kong to Huangshan takes two hours and from Seoul takes around four hours.

Shuttle buses 218 and 219 between the airport and Huangshan University operate every day at 2~3 CNY/person. A transfer is needed and the buses run 06:30–17:30 every 20 minutes.

A taxi service is provided on the first floor of the terminal building in the airport. A ride to the city center costs about 15~20 CNY in 10 minutes.

By Train:

There is one High-speed railway station in the city. Huangshanbei Railway Station (黄山北火车站) has trains from most big and medium cities across China, such as Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, and Guangzhou. A ride from Beijing needs 5.5 hours. The train from Shanghai takes 2.5 hours and from Hangzhou takes 1.5 hours.

Buses 2 stop in front of the railway station. There are direct buses running between the railway station and Huangshan University. They operate 07:05–21:30, departing every 30 minutes, and costing 5 CNY. A ride to Huangshan University is about 50 minutes.

A taxi service is also provided and A ride to Huangshan University costs about 45~70 CNY in 25 minutes.
