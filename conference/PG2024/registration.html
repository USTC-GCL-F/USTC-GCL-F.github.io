Note

  • At least one author from each paper/poster must have one full registration for the conference before the registration deadline for your paper/poster to be accepted.

  • Full and student registration includes all technical sessions, coffee breaks, and dining (3 lunches and 3 dinners), accommodation or travel is not included in the registration fee.

  • To take advantage of member registration rates, you must be an AG or a CCF member and provide a valid Membership ID number at the time you register.

  • Student ID Cards will be required on-site as evidence of the reduced student fee.

  • Cancellations are required to be made up to 15 days (on/before September 28, 2024) in advance. Otherwise, no refund will be issued.

Cancellation Policy

Any booking that is cancelled before (including) September 28, 2024 will be fully refunded. After this date no refund will be available. Please email pg2024@mail.hsu.edu.cn to make a cancellation request. The organisers cannot accept liability for costs incurred in the event of the conference having to be cancelled as a result of circumstances beyond its reasonable control.

Official Invitation Letter

If you need a visa to visit China, please fill in the fields of "OFFICIAL INVITATION LETTER FOR VISA" in the registration form, and provide the passport number, your name on the passport, and your paper ID. We will then send you the official invitation letter for visa application.
